Welcome to the Health Centre
at ABV-IIITM Gwalior

The Health Centre is committed to promoting the health and wellness of the campus community by providing a comprehensive range of prevention, education, and treatment services. Our dedicated team of medical professionals ensures that students, faculty, and staff receive the highest quality care in a compassionate and confidential environment.

Located in A block of ABV-IIITM Gwalior, the Health Centre is easily accessible and equipped with modern medical facilities to address both routine and urgent healthcare needs. We offer a variety of services, including general medical consultations, nursing care, emergency first aid, and diagnostic services. Our center is designed to ensure privacy and comfort, with separate consultation rooms, a well-maintained general ward, and specialized medical equipment.

In addition to treatment, the Health Centre places a strong emphasis on preventive care and health education. We regularly organize health awareness programs, vaccination drives, and wellness workshops to encourage healthy living among the campus community. Whether it's managing stress, preventing illness, or dealing with an emergency, the Health Centre is here to support the well-being of everyone at ABV-IIITM Gwalior.

Our mission is to foster a healthy campus environment where everyone can thrive academically, socially, and physically. We are committed to providing timely and effective healthcare services that cater to the unique needs of our diverse campus population.

Medical Center Exterior

The entrance of our college's health care center. With a covered parking area for ambulances and a clear sign indicating the facility, it ensures quick and easy access for students and staff in need of medical attention.

Nursing Room

The nursing room within the medical center. Equipped with essential medical supplies, this room provides a comfortable space for initial assessments and minor treatments, ensuring privacy and care for patients.

Observation Ward

the observation ward, designed for monitoring and short-term care. The ward features examination beds and essential medical equipment, offering a quiet and clean environment for students who need to rest or undergo observation.

Patient Room

A patient room with a comfortable bed and medical equipment. This room is used for students who require rest or short-term medical care under the supervision of the medical team.

Medical Equipment

Essential medical equipment used for diagnostic tests and treatments. The center is equipped with modern tools to ensure accurate assessments and effective care for patients.

General Ward

The general ward area features multiple beds separated by curtains for privacy. It is designed for students who need to be observed or treated for a short period.

wheelchair Area

The wheelchair storage area ensures mobility assistance is readily available for patients who need it. The medical center prioritizes accessibility and support for all students and staff.

Meeting Room

A meeting room within the medical center used for consultations, discussions, and staff meetings. It provides a comfortable setting for important conversations related to patient care and medical services.
